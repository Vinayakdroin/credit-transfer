Bir Tikendrajit University (BTU)

Imphal, Manipur

Bir Tikendrajit University (BTU), located in Imphal, Manipur, holds several recognitions to ensure its academic credibility and relevance. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and established by the State Government of Manipur.

UGC Recognized
AIU Member
BCI Approved
PCI Approved

BTU is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU), ensuring high standards of education, research, and governance while fostering relationships with universities across the country.

The Global University

Arunachal Pradesh

The Global University, Arunachal Pradesh, is an emerging educational institution that aims to provide quality education in various disciplines. The university is established by the state Government of Arunachal Pradesh, and its programs and degrees are recognized at the state and national levels.

UGC Recognition
State Approved
BCI Approved

The university seeks accreditation from recognized national and international bodies to ensure academic excellence and recognition of their degrees.

Maharaja Agrasen Himalayan Garhwal University (MAHGU)

Uttarakhand

Maharaja Agrasen Himalayan Garhwal University (MAHGU) is established and incorporated by Government of Uttarakhand Act No. 33 of 2016. Located in the peaceful Garhwal ranges of Shivalik hills, the university provides quality education with a focus on employment-oriented learning.

UGC Section 2(f) Recognized
Government of Uttarakhand Established
Bar Council of India Approved
Pharmacy Council of India Approved

Understanding University Accreditations

What these recognitions mean for your education

UGC
University Grants Commission

Recognition from UGC ensures the university meets required standards set by the Government of India and allows awarding of nationally recognized degrees.

NAAC
National Assessment and Accreditation Council

NAAC accreditation indicates quality assurance and continuous improvement in higher education institutions.

AIU
Association of Indian Universities

AIU membership ensures adherence to high standards and provides networking opportunities with universities across India.
