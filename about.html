About PIMT

Dedicated to providing flexible, career-driven education that bridges the gap between traditional education and industry needs

Our Story

Principle Institute of Management and Technology (PIMT) is dedicated to providing flexible, career-driven education that bridges the gap between traditional education and industry needs.

It traces its roots back to the establishment of Career Line Academy in Kerala in 2010, with a vision to empower underprivileged communities through education and skill development, aiming to address social and economic disparity.

Our commitment extends beyond conventional education, focusing on creating pathways for students who need alternative routes to academic success.

PIMT Services LLP Formation

In 2020, PIMT Services LLP was officially formed, focusing on credit transfer programs for degree and master's dropouts, alongside online and work-integrated learning programs.

These initiatives help working professionals gain both practical experience and academic qualifications. Additionally, PIMT offers apprenticeship-embedded degree programs, B.Voc & M.Voc courses, and skill-based training, further enhancing career prospects.

Key Offerings:
  • Credit transfer programs
  • Online learning programs
  • Work-integrated learning
  • Apprenticeship-embedded degrees
  • B.Voc & M.Voc courses
  • Skill-based training

Strategic Partnership

To support PIMT's growth, Bodha Corporate Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2025.

Bodha provides strategic direction, business insights, and industry partnerships, allowing PIMT to expand its offerings and become a leader in higher education and vocational training.

This partnership ensures that PIMT remains at the forefront of educational innovation, continuously adapting to industry needs and market demands.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

PIMT's mission is to create industry-ready professionals, blending theoretical knowledge with practical experience, and empowering students and professionals for success in an increasingly competitive job market.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in higher education and vocational training, recognized for bridging the gap between academia and industry while empowering communities through accessible, quality education.
