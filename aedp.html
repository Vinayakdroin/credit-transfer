Apprenticeship Embedded Degree Program (AEDP)

A revolutionary approach to education that combines academic learning with practical industry experience, aligned with National Education Policy (NEP) 2020

What is AEDP?

Apprenticeship-Embedded Degree Programs are a key component of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical industry experience.

These programs integrate on-the-job training with formal education, ensuring that students are not only equipped with theoretical knowledge but also hands-on skills that are directly relevant to the workforce. The goal is to enhance employability and prepare students for the demands of modern industries.

Aligned with NEP 2020

Our AEDP program embodies the vision of National Education Policy 2020

Blended Learning Approach

NEP 2020 emphasizes a blended learning approach, combining classroom-based education with real-world experience through apprenticeships, internships, and industry partnerships.

Credit-Based System

Students can earn academic credits for their apprenticeship training, making it a formal part of their curriculum. This ensures that apprenticeships are integrated into the academic structure.

Industry-Academia Collaboration

The policy promotes industry-academia collaboration, encouraging universities and colleges to partner with industries to create apprenticeship opportunities.

Key Benefits of AEDP

Why our apprenticeship-embedded approach makes a difference

Guaranteed Industry Training

Upon successful completion of the degree program, PIMT SERVICES LLP guarantees assured apprenticeship training in leading companies across Kerala. This opportunity allows students to work with prominent firms, applying their academic knowledge to industry settings.

Assured Stipend

In addition to gaining valuable work experience, students receive an assured stipend during their apprenticeship period. This financial support enables students to focus on their training without worrying about their expenses.

Enhanced Employability

The apprenticeship provides exposure to practical industry operations, helping students understand market demands and refine their skills to meet industry standards, significantly enhancing their employability.

Industry-Ready Professionals

By combining formal education with on-the-job training, PIMT helps students become industry-ready professionals, equipped with the skills, knowledge, and real-world experience highly sought after by employers.

NSQF Alignment

The National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF) further supports our AEDP approach by aligning educational qualifications with specific skills and competencies.

This makes apprenticeship programs an essential part of skill development, ensuring that students acquire not just theoretical knowledge but also practical competencies that are recognized at the national level.
